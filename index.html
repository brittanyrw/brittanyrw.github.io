about me

Brittany Walker is a web developer, instructor and musical theater fan. Currently she works at Social Driver, a Washington DC digital agency, where she builds websites. As a Front End Lead for Women Who Code DC and an Instructor for a free weekly coding workshop for Thinkful, she is very involved in the DC tech community. Brittany also is the co-founder of What the CSS?!, a fun course that teaches CSS via quirky challenges and regularly mentors for those learning how to code.
